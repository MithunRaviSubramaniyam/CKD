About Us

Welcome to SKCET, a leading institution dedicated to making advancements in healthcare technology. Our focus is on developing cutting-edge solutions that address pressing medical challenges.

Our mission is to predict Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) at an early stage using advanced data analytics and machine learning. Through our efforts, we aim to improve early detection, provide timely intervention, and ultimately save lives by helping individuals manage their kidney health better.
