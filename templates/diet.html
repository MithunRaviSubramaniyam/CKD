• CKD Meal Recommendations

Breakfast Options

1. Oatmeal with berries
Details: Rich in fiber and antioxidants, oatmeal helps maintain heart health. Pair with fresh berries for added nutrients.

2. Scrambled eggs with spinach
Details: Eggs provide high-quality protein, while spinach is loaded with iron and vitamins.

3. Whole grain toast with avocado
Details: A great source of healthy fats, fiber, and vitamins. Avocado can help regulate blood pressure.

Lunch Options

1. Grilled chicken salad
Details: Lean protein from chicken and a variety of vegetables provide essential nutrients.

2. Quinoa with steamed vegetables
Details: Quinoa is a complete protein, and steaming vegetables helps retain their nutrients.

3. Lentil soup
Details: High in protein and fiber, lentils are great for heart health and digestion.

Dinner Options

1. Baked salmon with asparagus
Details: Salmon is rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which are beneficial for heart health, while asparagus is high in fiber.

2. Stir-fried tofu with broccoli
Details: Tofu is a great source of plant-based protein, and broccoli is packed with vitamins.

3. Brown rice with vegetables
Details: Brown rice offers more fiber than white rice and complements the nutrients from mixed vegetables.
